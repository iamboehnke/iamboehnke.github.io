What the data says

Silence is the norm. Nearly half of concluded applications received no reply. This is not a reflection of fit — it is a structural feature of how hiring works here.

The funnel compresses fast. The drop-off between applying and being seen is steep, and mostly invisible to the applicant.

Language did not hurt English applications. Contrary to expectation, English-language applications had a slightly higher response rate than Danish-language ones. The likely explanation: English roles often go to larger, more professionally managed HR teams.
